Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of this website?

The purpose of this website is to cover the Houston Rockets organization through articles regarding news, rumors, and more. At Clutch City Link, my goal is to inform like-minded fans about everything surrounding the team. For more information on this topic, please visit the about me page.

How can I support the growth and sustainability of this website?

You can support this website by reading the articles posted and telling others about us as well. Additionally, you can follow @Clutchcitylink on social media platforms such as Youtube, Instagram, and Twitter. If you are a consistent enjoyer of the content posted on this website, you may consider donating to this platform in order to ensure its continued development.

Are the articles free to read?

Every article on this website is 100% free to enjoy. The option is available for you to donate to this website, which makes it easier and more sustainable for me to produce free content.

How often are new articles released?

New articles are released whenever there is a topic that is worth covering. It does take time to write and upload articles, so, most commonly, new stories will be available 2-3 times per week.

Is there a way to make an account for this website?

Currently, no account system has been implemented and all articles are available to anyone, but there are plans for expansion with a system as such being a top priority. New features will be added as the site gains traction.

Do I have to donate to read articles?

Although all donations are very much appreciated, they are not mandatory in any way to have access to the articles on this website.

Got any more questions? Feel free to contact me.
